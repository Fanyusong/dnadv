Da Nang Street Food Tour

Published on March 15, 2024 by Da Nang Adventure

Discover the Unique Flavors of Central Vietnam

Known as the most livable city in Vietnam, Da Nang is not only famous for its stunning natural landscapes but also for its rich and diverse culinary scene. To fully experience the distinctive flavors of this city, join the Da Nang Street Food Tour, a 3.5 to 4-hour journey exploring the signature street foods of Central Vietnam.

What Makes This Tour Special?

The Da Nang Street Food Tour is designed for food lovers who want to experience authentic local dishes. Instead of popular dishes like mì quảng or bún bò, which can be found everywhere, we will take you to hidden spots and small eateries tucked away in alleys where locals usually dine. This is your chance to discover unique dishes that many tourists often miss.

Dishes You Will Enjoy:

  • Grilled Pork Vermicelli: A local favorite, this dish combines fragrant grilled pork, fresh herbs, and soft vermicelli noodles, all topped with a tangy and sweet fish sauce.
  • Fish Cake Noodles (Bánh Canh Chả Cá): Enjoy a warm bowl of fish cake noodles, where soft, chewy noodles blend with flavorful fish cakes in a rich and savory broth.
  • Rice Paper Rolls: You'll have the chance to roll your own rice paper with fresh ingredients, accompanied by a traditional dipping sauce, creating a memorable and delicious experience.
  • Crispy Cakes (Bánh Đập): A crunchy street food treat made from rice flour, bánh đập offers a simple yet flavorful taste that reflects the rustic charm of Central Vietnam.

Throughout the journey, you'll not only savor these delicious dishes but also hear the stories and traditions behind them. Our guides will share fascinating insights into the origins of Vietnamese street food and the cooking methods that have been passed down through generations.

Unwind with Beer, Ice Cream, and Scenic Beauty

After your culinary adventure, relax with a cold beer or indulge in a refreshing ice cream. The tour doesn't end with food—we'll take you on a motorbike ride through Art Alley, a street art haven, and across Da Nang's iconic bridges, where you can admire the city's sparkling lights at night.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: 3.5 - 4 hours
  • Included in tour:
    • Pick up and drop off in Da Nang center or up to 4-5km away
    • 5-6 different local dishes at 4-5 main food stops
    • Professional English-speaking guide
    • One local beer (for guests 18+) or soft drink
    • Rain poncho (if needed)
    • Vegetarian options available
  • Excluded in Tour
    • Other personal expenses.
    • Tips and gratuities (optional)

With us, a street food tour is not just about eating; it's about discovering the culture, history, and lifestyle of the locals. The Da Nang Street Food Tour offers you a unique and unforgettable journey through the heart of the city.
