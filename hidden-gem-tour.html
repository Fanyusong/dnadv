Hidden Gems Tour

Published on March 15, 2024 by Da Nang Adventure

Unearth Da Nang's Best-Kept Secrets

Step off the beaten path and uncover Da Nang's most cherished and untouched treasures with our Hidden Gems Tour. This carefully curated journey takes you to destinations that are often overlooked by mainstream tourists, offering a glimpse into the city's pristine natural landscapes and deep-rooted cultural heritage. Unlike typical travel itineraries, this tour invites you to explore the authentic soul of Da Nang—places that remain relatively unspoiled, preserving their traditional charm and breathtaking beauty.

Tour Highlights:

Your journey begins at the breathtaking Sơn Trà Peninsula, Da Nang's "green lung," where winding roads lead you through untouched forests and offer sweeping ocean views. This natural sanctuary is home to the famous red-shanked douc langur and provides a peaceful escape from the city. A visit to Ký ức Làng Chài (Fishermen's Memory House) takes you into the heart of Da Nang's fishing heritage, where a unique house built from old boats and fishing gear tells the nostalgic story of the local fishing community, all set within lush, serene surroundings. The adventure continues at Cồn Market, one of Da Nang's most iconic and lively markets, where the rich aromas of local dishes like bánh canh chả cá (fish cake noodles) fill the air. Here, you can engage with friendly vendors and experience the true flavors of the city. After the market, unwind at a traditional Vietnamese café, sipping on bold, aromatic drip coffee in a cozy, authentic setting that invites you to slow down and soak in the local atmosphere. Finally, a visit to Mural Village immerses you in a vibrant world of street art, where colorful, hand-painted murals transform the walls into a living canvas, celebrating the creativity and culture of Da Nang.

Tour Details:

  • Duration: 3.5 - 4 hours
  • Included in tour:
    • Pick up and drop off in Da Nang center or up to 4-5km away
    • Professional English-speaking guide and guide.
    • One item of local beer ( above 18 years old) or soft drink.
    • Rain poncho (if needed)

    • 2 different local dishes and 1 local coffee.
  • Excluded in Tour
    • Other personal expenses.
    • Tips and gratuities (optional)

The Hidden Gems Tour is perfect for travelers seeking new and unique experiences beyond typical tourist spots. Join us in uncovering Da Nang's hidden treasures!
